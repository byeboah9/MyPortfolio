Travis Bickle is a complex character to analyze, however his intention throughtout the film are clear. He a man
who is desperate to find someone to keep him company whether it be a girlfriend, co-worker, or a reliable person he can depend on. In the beginning of the film
we know Travis's background and characteristics he still believe in a better future for him and his surrondings. When he first see Betsy for the first time
Travis felt a potential of long-lasting love and comfort, especially needed in the harsh streets of NYC. It even translates with his support of presidential candidate
Charles Palantine, as Betsy is a campaign worker for him. After a date gone wrong with Betsy and her seeming very uninterested with him, Travis is back to the same state as he
was before, but now more angry and sad with his situation. He has no one to help or deeply talk to as his job is there to pay the bills, and his co-workers are more like acquintances.
His motivate changes after an incident where a skipliy clad girl comes to after taxi out of nowhere and asked him to hurry and leave. However, she is stopped by
a mysterious man where he gives Travis some money to compensate for the distrubance in his car, and takes the girl with him. Feeling a little complicit about
doing nothing to help the girl, Travis works while trying to find the girl who was in his car wanting help. Eventually getting finding her location, he discovers
that this young girl is a postitute and the man who took out of the car is her pimp. Disgusted with the siutation going on Travis is set on helping this young girl (named Iris)
to escape her predictment. The two become somewhat friends and he made an agreement with her to give her money to left her job and move far away from there. Whilst this happens
Travis is even more mental destroy with see what unmoralism seeing in front of his eyes, and feels that the people in power are there to blame, so
decides to plan to kill Charles Palantine. When he failed to asssassinate him and luckliy escapes, and changes his plan to get Iris out of place by force.
